# 🚀 Intro to Programming & Basics of Computation

Welcome to this comprehensive guide designed to ground you in the fundamentals of computing and software construction. 

### 📝 Overview

*   **Self-Contained Revision Guide:** A complete, standalone resource tailored for computer science students and tech enthusiasts looking to solidify their conceptual understanding.
*   **First-Hand Experience:** Built entirely on direct, practical learning experiences to bridge the gap between abstract theory and real-world execution.

## 🧭 Core Focus Areas

*   **Computational Thinking:** Breaking down complex problems into step-by-step algorithms.
*   **Syntax & Logic:** Mastering variables, conditional control flows, and iterative loops.
*   **Data Structures:** Understanding how data is stored, organized, and manipulated efficiently.
*   **Execution Mechanics:** Learning how hardware processes instructions written in high-level code.
